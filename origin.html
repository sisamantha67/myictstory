Origin and My Thoughts about the Story

According to Filipino folklore, the myth of Santelmo is believed to have originated from people who witnessed mysterious floating lights at night near swamps, rivers, and forests. Long ago, travelers and fishermen had no modern lighting, so these strange flames were often blamed for people getting lost or disappearing. Some believed Santelmo was the spirit of a sinful person or a lost soul punished to wander forever, while others thought it was a warning created by nature itself. Over time, these stories were passed down orally to teach children and travelers to be careful when walking alone at night. Personally, I think the Santelmo legend is important because it shows how Filipinos used stories to explain things they could not understand.
