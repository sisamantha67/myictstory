The End

Tomas stopped, prayed for help, and turned away from the flame. Slowly, Santelmo faded into the darkness. By listening to the sound of the waves, Tomas found his way back home at dawn. From that night on, he respected the legend of Santelmo, learning that myths exist to teach people caution, respect for nature, and wisdom in the face of danger.
