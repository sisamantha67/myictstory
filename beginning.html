The Beginning

In a small coastal village in the Philippines, where wooden
houses stood on stilts and the sea stretched endlessly beyond the shore,
people often spoke in hushed voices about Santelmo. It was said to appear at night
as a floating ball of fire near rivers, forests, and lonely paths. A young fisherman
named Tomas lived in the village and had heard these stories all his life, especially
from his grandmother.
