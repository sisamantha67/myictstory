The Middle

One dark evening after fishing, Tomas walked home through the mangroves. Suddenly, a glowing flame appeared ahead of him, flickering like fire but floating in the air. Curious and tired, Tomas followed the light, believing it would guide him safely. As he walked farther, the path became dangerous and muddy, and he realized he was being led away from the village. Fear struck him as he remembered the warnings about Santelmo leading travelers astray.
